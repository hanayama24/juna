About

Hi, My name is Ellen or Alyona in my native language. My life is all about creativity in all different kinds of it. And, Yes! I’m a photographer for more than 8 years. I used to live in Ukraine and I worked on wedding photo and fashion at that time. I made marvellous wedding memories for more than 100 couples, I made photos for magazines and created portfolios for model. Also, I used to live and work in Germany, where I earned lots of experience and made many beautiful photos for my portfolio. Today, I’m working in Seattle and US, I’m not limited to that area and ready to travel. I’d love to create lots of new masterpieces and implement my creative ideas to live and give you unforgettable photos
